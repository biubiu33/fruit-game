Ninja Fruit Vision

A hand-tracking fruit slicing game driven by your camera. The fingertip of your index finger becomes the blade trail on the main stage.

The bottom-right panel always shows the live camera feed with hand skeleton lines.
The main canvas turns fast fingertip motion into a bright slicing trail.
Missed fruit costs lives, so keep the finger moving and chain combos.
Best results come from good lighting and a clear view of your hand.
